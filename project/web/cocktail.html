The Dirty Vienetta

Or: How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Stop Worrying

This cocktail contains alcohol, and as such is not suitable for those below 18 years of age, or the legal drinking age in your region or area.

Ingredients

  • 1 Part Irish Cream
  • 3 Parts Whole Milk
  • 1 Slice of a Ripe Banana (Optional)

Recipe

Pour the Irish cream and milk into a shaker, and then shake the ingredients to aerate them. Pour the drink into a tall glass, without ice, and — optionally — garnish the glass with a banana slice placed on the rim.
The drink should be served cold, or at room temperature.

About

The name of the cocktail stems from its similarity to melted ice cream, with dirty referring to the alcoholic nature of the drink.
