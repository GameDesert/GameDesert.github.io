How to force quit Roblox Cheat Suite:

If your copy of RCS is malfunctioning, (for example, the autoclicker not switching off), you can force quit the application in 3 steps to stop its running.

  1. Open Windows Security Options by pressing [Ctrl] + [Alt] + [Delete] and then press Task Manager
  2. Find and select Roblox Cheat Suite from the list of applications.
  3. Click end task to force quit the app. It should close and the autoclicker will be disabled.
