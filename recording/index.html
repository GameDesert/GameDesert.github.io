Oliver K's Home Recording Tips...

  • Try to dampen any unnecessary reverb and echo by placing pillows and rolled-up towels around your recording device.

  • Keep the microphone under arms-length from you.

  • Make sure there is no background noise and close doors and windows to avoid any outside noise.

  • Make sure the microphone is right in front of you.

  • You can use your phone to record, you don't need any expensive equipment.

  • Don't play too loud to avoid audio clipping.

  • Make sure you are always in frame.

  • Clean your camera lens before recording to avoid any smudges.
