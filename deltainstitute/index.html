Your Mission

Over the past year, 74 people have gone missing. Seemingly unrelated cases, we've found 1 link: They were all in contact with agents from the Delta Institute a week before they went missing.

You must break into the systems of the Delta Institute and uncover the dark truth of whatever horrible experiments they're doing on these innocent people before it's too late.


How To Play

  • This game is a text-based adventure. You will need to read through the text, view the images, click the buttons, and enter codes to complete it.
  • You will not need to type anything into your address bar, you will only need to use the links on the page.
  • Everything you will need to solve the puzzles is provided in the text, you will not need to view the page source, or have any prior knowledge.


Before You Start

Your journey will never lead you outside of the /deltainstitute directory unless otherwise stated.
