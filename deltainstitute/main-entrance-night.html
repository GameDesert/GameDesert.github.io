Main Entrance (Night Time)


A small van with blacked-out windows slows to a halt outside the ancient building.

You push the van's door forwards, slide it open, and step out onto the gravelled driveway with a crunch.

It's almost pitch-black outside, and under the cover of night, you walk towards the front door of the institute.

As you're walking, you notice a sliver of light coming from one of the windows and an eye watching you as you inch closer to the entrance.

You make eye contact with the figure and it disappears into the darkness of the institute.

Check the door.
